Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping, shipping, returns, and more.

Placing an order is simple:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click the cart icon when you're ready to checkout
  3. Enter your shipping information
  4. Choose your payment method
  5. Review your order and complete purchase

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, the order enters our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.

We accept multiple payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal and Venmo
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Zelle (direct bank transfer)
  • Cash App

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days) - $9.99
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days) - $19.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day) - $29.99
  • Free Standard Shipping on orders over $100

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package on our website using the "Track Order" feature, or directly on the carrier's website.

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items in original packaging. Items must be in the same condition as received, with all tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items.

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our support team with your order number
  2. We'll provide a return shipping label
  3. Package the item securely in original packaging
  4. Drop off at the designated shipping location
  5. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days
